  • Organizational forms of breakthrough technology creation:
    special economic zones; systematic territory management projects; important socio-economic, industrial, scientific and technological development projects, the implementation of which guarantees major shifts in the technological transformation of domestic production, increasing the share of competitive products of Russian enterprises on the domestic and global markets, as well as solving acute social problems.
  • Special Economic Zones:
    Areas that have an appropriate status supported by specific conditions. Established and developed with budgetary resources and are part of the national innovation policy of the state. They have well-developed transport, engineering and social infrastructure. The economic effect of their creation is estimated at tens billions rubles. Their main objectives include: creation of basic breakthrough technologies, which are the basis for the development and production of high-tech products that are competitive on foreign and domestic markets; development of innovation infrastructure; development of production and technical re-equipment of enterprises at a modern technical and technological level.
  • System projects for territorial management:
    a form of implementation of breakthrough technologies. Used when the climatic and territorial differences of the subjects are so great that the development of regional territorial management technologies and their integration into a complex systemic socio-technological complex at the federal level is a necessary condition for the development and preservation of the integrity of society.
  • Formation of breakthrough technologies:
    a special type of activity of the state and business, focused on satisfying the needs of the real sector of economy. Large-scale and effective development of breakthrough technologies is possible if this task is the basis of state policy that provides large-scale support for all participants in this process.
