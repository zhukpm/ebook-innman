  • Methods of working up group solutions of innovative organization development:
    research methods aimed at finding a practical solution to the problem of innovative organization: "Brainstorming" method (each participant of "brainstorming" contributes to the solution of the problem); expert method (evaluation of solutions is carried out by invited expert specialists, who combine the knowledge of the specifics of the examined object with knowledge in the field of management science); method of scenarios (consecutive events that bring the problem situation to the desired finite state with a certain probability are described in detail); Risk solutions (estimated the level of probability of achieving the results, failures and deviations from the predicted objectives through quantitative and qualitative risk analysis); intuitive decisions (based on intuition, which have managers who can foresee the future of the organization).
  • Rules for "brainstorming":
    The creative imagination of the participants is not restricted in any way and they can express any ideas; criticism and form of evaluation of the ideas expressed is not allowed. Everyone may supplement and develop considerations of any participant of meeting, but has no right to challenge them; the evaluation of scientific and practical importance of ideas is carried out after processing of materials of meeting. All ideas put forward are explored, even if their feasibility is questionable at the moment; points on which agreement is reached are recorded in the minutes of the meeting, signed by the chairperson and each participant; recommendations that for one reason or another cannot be implemented are structured for further refinement or presentation to a special meeting for discussion.
