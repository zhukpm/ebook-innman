  • Spiritual sphere
    is represented by the complex of formal and non-formal institutions in society. It is an organic part of integrity of life activity of a society, is inseparably connected with other components of this integrity and has essential influence on their advancement.
  • Spirituality
    the reflection of the world around us in the heart and mind of man. It is revealed on the basis of core values of the people arising from its culture, traditions and way of life. The core values serve as criteria of self-assessment of a story of the person.
  • Functions of the spiritual sphere
    1. preservation, enrichment, reproduction of the basic values of the people arising from its millenian culture and their use in all spheres of vital activity of the person (in personal life, family, industrial, social-political, public-power);
    2. mobilization of the people on execution of the purpose uniting it on achievement of true target orientations of development and consolidation of space of the state in the integral power, based on the uniform values;
    3. rendering of essential influence on forming of the public consciousness.
