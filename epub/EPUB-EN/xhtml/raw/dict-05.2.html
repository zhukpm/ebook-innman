  • Venture financing:
    it is a long-term credit given to innovation organization on loan (without providing guarantees). Increased risk of investor is compensated by higher interest rate (in comparison with the one given out in banks) and the probability of obtaining super-high profits in future.
  • Venture (risk) firms
    organizations that are created in the development and implementation of innovation, encouraging investors to invest in risky projects of creating technologies, products and services.
  • Engineering
    providing a set of separate types of technical and industrial services associated with research, design, construction, commissioning, development of new technological processes at the customer's facilities (with the improvement of existing production processes) on the basis of the contract. Engineering activity is subdivided on design and consulting, contracting and management. Engineering allows to summarize and use scientific and technical knowledge to solve technical and production problems.
