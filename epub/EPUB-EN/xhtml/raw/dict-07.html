  3. Small business. Financial investment funds

Topic summary

Innovation activities are carried out with the aim of investing capital to support and develop innovation. It can only be effective if it is adequately financed. Different sources of financing innovative activity are known. These are both state, commercial and non-commercial investment resources, resources of collective investors, insurance companies, credit institutions, commercial banks, budget funds, enterprises' own funds and many others. The system of innovative activity financing is a complex interweaving of forms and sources, differing by the type of property, the degree of centralization, forms of financing. The modern type of innovative investment is intellectual property, which gives an exclusive right to an individual or a legal entity to the results of intellectual activity and their investment as an investment.

The choice and use of the most effective sources and forms of financing for innovation should be motivated accordingly in each case.
