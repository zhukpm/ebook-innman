Conditions of the development of innovative management in the state sphere and business

  1. Key concepts
  2. Spiritual
  3. Industrial-economic
  4. Social and political, and governmental
Topic summary

Innovative management can be successfully formed only under certain conditions. In the state sphere and business, it is designed to serve the innovation sphere, the diffusion of innovations and the transformation of knowledge into concrete results. The diffusion and use of innovation leads to radical changes. Such changes are possible if the right spiritual, production and commercial, social, political and public power conditions are provided.
