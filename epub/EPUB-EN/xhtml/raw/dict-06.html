Topic summary

Innovation project is a system of interrelated objectives and programs for their implementation, containing a set of research, development, manufacturing, financial, commercial and other activities. The project allows effective management of executors and coordination of all types of resources, purposefully used throughout its life cycle. For this purpose, various methods of innovative management based on the corresponding normative-legal support are used. Implementation of the project depends on the sources and forms of financing innovations.
