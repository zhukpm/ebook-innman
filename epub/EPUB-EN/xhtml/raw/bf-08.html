Motivation of innovation activity

Keywords

  • Motives and incentives. Incentive is understood as an external cause that can cause an employee to have certain motives for the activity they are aware of. A motive is an internal stimulus that activates the directed activity of a person.
  • Motivation is a complex and dynamic process of motivating an employee (with the help of intrapersonal and external factors) to purposeful activities aimed at achieving innovation individual and organizational goals (setting and encouraging specific tasks).
  • Creative labor is the work of people who have the ability to analytical thinking, an increased sense of self-esteem and independence in actions. The success of the innovation process increasingly depends on their interested activities.
