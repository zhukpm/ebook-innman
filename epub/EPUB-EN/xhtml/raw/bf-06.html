Innovation project

Keywords

  • Innovation project is a system of interrelated goals and programs for their implementation, which is a set of research, development, production, financial, commercial and other targeted activities.
  • Project management is the name of an independent multi-profile discipline that combines special and supra-professional knowledge. Project management refers to the art of directing and coordinating all types of resources that are purposefully used throughout the project lifecycle.
  • System of programs and projects — interrelated elements of subsystems that form the basis of the program method of management. In turn, the main form of program management is targeted integrated programs based on the principles of program-target planning.
