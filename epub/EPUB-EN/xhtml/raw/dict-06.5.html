  • Advantages of innovative programs of innovative organization:
    providing rational interaction of departments, engaged in implementation of innovations; proximity of management bodies to executors; providing effective control over execution of works; rising responsibility of management bodies of organization and executors, engaged in development and implementation of innovations; release of managers from functions of operative management and coordination of actions of executors, engaged in creation and implementation of innovations.
  • Innovative target program:
    set of planned scientific and technical, industrial, economic, organizational and administrative actions, united by uniform purposeful process system "research - manufacture - management", covering functionally, and also on resources, terms and executors of all its interconnected stages.
  • Classification of innovation projects
    the grouping of innovation projects according to: the nature of the project objectives; the type of needs to be met; the type of innovation; the level of decisions to be made and the stages to be covered; the time of implementation.
  • Classification of targeted innovation programs
    distribution of targeted innovation projects by scale and level of interaction: innovation programs, which are implemented throughout the project with participation of a group of departments based on their use of centralized resources of the organization; inter-functional programmes, which temporarily involve one or two interacting departments that focus on solving the target problem with resources allocated to each of them.
  • Duties of the manager of targeted investment programs
    ensuring the implementation of decisions considered by the scientific and technical council of the top management; authorizing the technical and organizational decision taken in the process of interaction with co-executives, other units of the organization; operational coordination of work; assessing the implementation of the plan by the units.
