Innovation management solutions

Keywords

  • Management solution is a means of eliminating contradictions that arise in the process of developing an innovation organization. As is known, the factors of the organization's development are its internal contradictions, the accumulation of which threatens chaos and loss of management. Solution-making helps to structurally streamline the organization and bring it into the mainstream of managed development.
  • Problem situation — condition in which there is a discrepancy between the desired position of the organization and the really existing one, for the elimination of which a purposeful solution is developed and made in a timely manner with the involvement and use of the necessary resources.
  • Information support of the solution is the most important prerequisite for the development of good-quality scenarios of problem situations, determining the reasons and choosing the moment of solution-making. Timely processing of reliable information that characterizes the state of the organization's relationships with the external environment allows you to make reasoned decisions.
