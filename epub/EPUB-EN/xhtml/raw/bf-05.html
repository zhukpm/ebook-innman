Organizational forms of innovation management

Keywords

  • Small innovation firms are formations that have certain advantages over large companies in implementing innovative functions due to their inherent properties: initiative and staff cohesion, entrepreneurial spirit, willingness to take certain risks, and quick response to changes in the operational environment.
  • Technology polis — a research and production structure created on the basis of city acceptable for this or in its zone, in which economy technology parks play a significant role. The technology park is a research and production territorial complex with a complex functional structure designed to create the most favorable environment for the development of small science-intensive firms, essentially an incubator of technologies. The park includes various centers (research, innovation (scientific and technical complex), marketing, training center) and, of course, an industrial zone.
  • Venture (risk) firms — organizations that are created in the field of development and implementation of innovations that encourage investors to invest in risky projects to create technologies, products and services.
