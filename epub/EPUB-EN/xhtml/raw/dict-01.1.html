Chapter 1. Conditions of the development of innovative management in the state sphere and business

Key concepts

  • Business
    proactive, innovative, independent activity under conditions of risk, carried out under personal responsibility in order to gain profit or satisfaction from the achieved result.
  • State sphere
    is the sphere of realization of state functions in society. Directly or indirectly, it applies to spiritual, production and commercial, socio-political and public-power sphere.
  • Innovation in the state sphere and business
    is a transformation of potential progress into real, embodied in new technologies, products and services of state sphere and business.
  • Innovative technologies in the state sphere and business
    technologies for creation, distribution, implementation and application of innovations that improve the quality and efficiency of the realization of basic functions in these areas of activity.
  • Innovative manager of a new generation
    a highly educated state official and businessman, strong in spirit and loving Russia, with the appropriate gift of nature and possessing breakthrough management technologies. This is not only a profession, but also a calling. It is necessary to have an innovative mind, intuition, ingenuity and other extraordinary qualities and to be oriented towards using innovative knowledge for the benefit of the people, based on the interests of the state, society and business, compatible with the laws of conscience.
  • Innovation management in the state sphere and business
    the science about the art of management, based on the spiritual values of the people and innovative technologies that increase the efficiency of management in these areas of activity, the quality of their interaction and the results of functioning.
  • The innovation sources in the state sphere and business
    culture, transformation, renewal and change, new knowledge.
  • Classification of socio-economic functions of the state
    functions of general order (typical for the state of all times and peoples); special functions (specific to the state at a certain stage of development of society and national economy); single (particular) functions of the state, representing the historical originality of its development, mentality of society and necessity to resolve its vital problems.
  • General, basic functions of the state

    providing the balance of economic interests of subjects of economic and socio-political life of the company; providing the conditions of life of the company as a whole and the industrial-economics sphere in particular.

    In modern conditions, these functions are concretized in the following specific functions: ensuring the legal framework and the social atmosphere necessary for the effective functioning of the market economy; protection of competition; support of socially fair distribution of incomes; adjustment of the distribution of resources in order to change the structure of the national product; stabilization of the economy, i.e. control of the employment rate and inflation, generated by economic fluctuations; stimulation of economic growth.

  • General live spheres of the society
    spiritual, industrial-economics, socio-political, public-power. There is an appropriate division of labor between them. All marked areas together make up an integral system of society's vital activity and are in a state of constant interaction. The area of this interaction is extensive and affects, first of all, socio-political and economic interests of the parties.
  • Subjects and objects of innovative management
    the public sphere, business, society and the system of their interaction. The state is influencing on all life spheres of society for their balanced development. Business provides income in conditions created for it by the state. The society is between business and government, defending its inherent interests. Innovation management acts as a modern tool to implement management technologies to balance interests in the most advantageous way.
  • Conditions of development of innovative management in the state sphere and business
    the general requirements developed by spiritual, industrial-economics, socio-political and public spheres of life of the society, each of which performs its specific function.
  • Functions of the state

    the basic directions of activity of the state, allowing it to play a relevant role in society.

    In the advanced civil society, the functions of the state are concentrated on: formulation of the general strategy of development; identification and substantiation of priorities, rates, proportions of the growth of economic and social spheres of society; stimulation of socially useful activity of citizens; protection of their rights, property and personal dignity; democratization of all spheres of life of people; protection of borders and provision of necessary internal order.
