Nature and goals of the decision

  • Prerequisites necessary for the development of effective innovative solutions:
    the presence of a social component in the solution; the availability of legal, resource and information support for solutions; the development of alternative solutions; the formation of motivation and responsibility systems for the quality of solutions; the availability of implementation and control mechanism for solutions; scientific validity of solutions; priority consideration of external environment factors; the probability of solutions according to the adopted quality, cost and timing criteria.
  • The requirements for the management decision:
    the rapid transformation of innovative solutions into reality; timeliness; alternativeness; reliability; targeting; consistency; eligibility and directivity; completeness; clear goals, specific timelines, the main ways and means of its implementation.
  • Managerial solution:
    an act of will of the manager used as a means of eliminating internal contradictions arising in the process of development of an innovative organization, the accumulation of which threatens chaos and loss of management. The decision-making process streamlines the structure of the organization and brings it into manageable development. The purpose of the decision is to mobilize and focus all available resources to bring the innovative organization into a better state (in line with its goals and objectives).
  • Decision-making function
    the most significant function in the activity of the manager of innovative organization. It includes the whole process of innovative management, completing it after corresponding adjustment, and its quality and effectiveness are the most important components of success of a modern organization.
