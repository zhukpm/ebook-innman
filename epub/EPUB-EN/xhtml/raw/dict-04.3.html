  • Basic functions of innovation management
    types of activity, inherent in all organizations, without them it is impossible their effective functioning. There are private and specific functions, necessity of which depends on peculiarities of organization (its affiliation, purpose, ownership form, size, etc.). The innovative organization, as any other, carries out general functions of analysis, forecasting, planning, organization, motivation, adaptation, coordination, account, control and estimation. All of them in aggregate make up functional structure of innovative management.
  • Function of the analysis
    the cognitive activity of the head or pro-professional work of analytical services of organizations. There are different methods of analysis. The most common methods are comparative analysis, factor analysis and economic-mathematical methods. Comparative analysis reveals the differences among comparable objects of the innovations. The method of factor analysis is characterized by the fact that it is based on determining the level of influence of specific factors on the efficiency. Economical and mathematical methods of analysis are supposed to select the most efficient (optimal by a given criterion) variants of economic decisions in existing (planned) conditions of activity.
  • Forecasting function
    is understood as a scientifically reasoned judgment about the possible alternative ways and timing of the development of the subject of innovation, the results of which are expressed in the forecast. Forecast serves as a pre-planning document that contains quantitative characteristics (estimates) of the object of management, the expected timing, volumes, costs, quality of work and other related indicators. All of them are probabilistic and further need to be adjusted.
  • Purpose of forecasting
    determination of scientifically grounded (reliable) tendencies of development of innovation object, which will be used during development of strategic plans in general, but first of all during research and development works. The purpose is realized through the main tasks of forecasting: determination of tendencies (economic, scientific, technical), capable to influence satisfaction of needs of consumers; study and forecast of market demand.
  • Planning function
    the most important function of management, which determines specific, reasoned goals and objectives, develops methods and means of their implementation. The plan and the forecast are mutually complementary stages of management (with the defining role of the plan). Unlike the forecast, the plan contains not alternative, but unequivocal characteristics of the planned object, based on rational forecast variant. The plan serves as a basis of innovative organizations activity, providing coordinated work of their subdivisions and determination of need in resources.
  • Coordination function
    is called to provide achievement of coordination in activity of all conjugated links of the organization by means of establishment between them of rational communications. Widely used means of communication (reports, meetings, conferences, documents, etc.) allow to establish links and interactions between subsystems of innovative organizations, to ensure the unity and coherence of all stages and functions of the management process.
  • Objectives of product innovation management:
    examination of the conformity degree of product parameters to the idea and mission of the organization; market research of innovative products; determination of ways to sell the product innovation; analysis of risks and search of possibilities of their minimization; forecasting of activity in the context of the stage of life cycle of the organization; determination of purposefulness of cooperation with competing organizations.
