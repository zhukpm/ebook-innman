  • Legal methods classification used in innovation management:
    administrative legal methods which are fixed by norms of administrative law; civil legal methods fixed by norms of labor law; labor methods fixed by norms of civil law; financial legal and other methods fixed by norms of corresponding sectors of national economy.
  • Communications in the innovative organization
    is a complex multilevel system of connections, covering the organization itself, its internal communications between departments, and external environment - relations with competitors, clients, creditors, tax service. In its turn, the internal communications are divided into vertical and horizontal.
  • Legal support in innovation management:
    1. legal questions of economy of the country (development of enterprise in various spheres, tax system, financial and credit policy, foreign trade activities);
    2. laws and regulations on standardization, metrology, certification of goods and services, consumer rights protection, antimonopoly policy, safety and labor protection;
    3. laws and regulations on regulation of resource-saving, production development, social development, environmental protection;
    4. legal regulation of formation and functioning of
  • Main objectives of legal support of innovative organizations activity:
    protection of the rights and interests of the organization in interaction with public authorities, judicial and law enforcement authorities, municipal institutions, contractors; use of legal means for effective business and realization of the profit purpose; legal support of corporate events; ensuring legal means for safety and acquisition of property, proper performance of contractual obligations; local lawmaking and ensuring the legality of organizational and managerial documentation; building a legal culture.
  • Law in innovative management:
    is the set of norms or rules of conduct, on which the head, manager and ordinary worker, seeking order and orderliness in the organization, ensuring the unconditional priority of law in the regulation of management relations.
  • Legal support of innovation management
    includes various legislative and normative acts adopted at the federal and regional levels. These acts include existing federal laws, resolutions of the government and federal governing bodies, state regulations and standards.
