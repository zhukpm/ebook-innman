Topic summary

Innovation is managed through an innovative approach with the aim of achieving the best commercial and social results through a functional or process approach. Innovation management is based on private and specific functions that depend on the needs of the organization. These special features determine the choice of organizational forms of innovation management that allow the most complete realization of innovation possibilities.
