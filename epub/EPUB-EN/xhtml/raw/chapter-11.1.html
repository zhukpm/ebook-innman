Breakthrough innovation technologies

1. The concept of breakthrough technologies

There are various ways to achieve socio-economic goals. The most popular of them are those that allow the organization to achieve impressive results in a short time (with a significant lead over its competitors). Among such methods, there are known breakthrough technologies that arise in the process of human development, as they acquire new knowledge that forms the basis of a new qualitative round of improvement of civilization. Countries in which modern technologies are not a decisive factor for development are increasingly lagging behind societies that create breakthrough technologies and knowledge-intensive products in terms of population well-being.

It is useful to highlight innovations that symbolize major inventions and become the basis for the formation of new generations and areas of technology that are recognized as breakthrough.

Breakthrough technologies are a product of the national innovation system and innovation management, an important factor not only in social but also in economic growth. The material goods and services produced with their help usually do not have competition in the world market and are characterized by mass demand, wide export opportunities, develop high-tech sectors of the national economy, allow you to get high profits, raise the prestige of the country.

Breakthrough are considered innovations (a product, service, or change in strategy) that lead to a significant increase in the organization's revenue and net profit. The exact definition of the term "innovation-breakthrough" is very difficult to give. It is necessary to consider and disclose the essence of each specific innovation-breakthrough separately.

The results of implementing a breakthrough technology are always obvious and indisputable. Breakthrough innovation involves the creation of new directions of activity (problem solving and meeting needs, or even the formation of such). The creation of innovations of this type requires appropriate personnel - innovators of high qualification, purposeful concentration of efforts, the formation of a favorable intellectual environment. The main thing here is creative thinking, free from stereotypes. In addition, highly effective innovation activity, coupled with penetration into the field of breakthrough technologies, is impossible without intuitive solutions.

Breakthrough technology is such a concentration of energy of various knowledge that, under certain conditions, "break" the usual (traditional) ideas about an object, opening up space for fundamentally new opportunities for its qualitative transformation.

Breakthrough technologies are implemented on the basis of:

  • concentrating the intellectual potential of specialists in various fields and branches of science, technology and production in the most priority scientific and technical directions;
  • new physical principles, unconventional methods and technical solutions to current scientific and technical problems of the country's economy and security;
  • highly effective organizational and methodological forms of interaction between the structural divisions of the organization and with external partners, including research and production and other teams of the country. Breakthrough technologies include a set of production processes, a scientific description of production methods or management methods that have no analogues in the world and lead to infusionIt is necessary to achieve revolutionary changes in various spheres of people's life activity. They are born in a certain environment, in the appropriate conditions for this. The basic source of breakthrough technologies is culture, the resulting values and accumulated knowledge of humanity, which allow the state to create special conditions that contribute to their formation and implementation.

Breakthrough technologies are also born out of new scientific paradigms, new knowledge that extends not only to technical and technological, but also to social, organizational, and economic areas. New knowledge allows you to create structures that serve breakthrough technologies in the field of economics, politics, and business management. They provide an opportunity to form and develop an organizational mechanism for combining efforts in this direction.

All breakthrough technologies can be divided into two groups: forming and destroying the system. The breakthrough technologies that form the system should correspond to the general cultural values of civilization and not contradict them. Often they are born at the intersection of cultures of peoples and their basis is the innovative achievements of these cultures. such time-tested technologies serve as the basis for the development and survival of peoples.

In its turn, destructive technologies ignore cultural values, come into conflict with them and are focused on extracting super-profits.

Breakthrough technologies can be critical and dual-use. Critical technologies are those that are most important from the point of view of state needs and public interests. Critical technologies can be identified in each of the priority areas of science and technology development. They are cross-sectoral in nature and create significant prerequisites for the development of many technological areas.

Among the critical priority for the development of the national economy, include those technical artifacts, projects and technologies, which can radically to influence the improvement of quality of life, health promotion and solution of social problems. They contribute to increasing employment, level and quality of education, improve the system of state, municipal management, commercial awareness, public organizations and individual citizens contribute to the stabilization of the political situation.

These technologies, which have radical commercial and social significance, include communication technologies, continental and transcontinental transport systems, energy technologies, environmental technologies, as well as those that affect the health of the population and demographic dynamics. They also include social technologies related to the management of megacities and the agro-industrial complex.

Developed countries define a list of technological directions and developments that are supported by the state in the interests of economic and military superiority. Their selection is carried out on the basis of a thorough and multi-stage procedure, including expertise from scientists, financiers and business leaders, analysts of reputable organizations and professional politicians.

There are several priority areas for the development of critical technologies at the federal level. Among them are information technologies and electronics, manufacturing technologies, new materials and chemical products, fuel and energy. Within these areas, the following critical technologies were selected: atomic and hydrogen energy; catalysts; composites; laser technologies; multiprocessor electronic computers with a parallel structure; non-traditional technologies for processing solid fuels and uranium; polymers; mathematical modeling systems; speech, text and image recognition and synthesis systems; technologies for deep processing of mining and man-made raw materials using non-traditional methods; technologies for studying the subsurface, forecasting, prospecting, exploration of mineral and uranium reserves; technologies for monitoring the natural and man-made sphere; technologies for the regeneration of spent nuclear fuel, utilization and disposal of radioactive waste; electron-ion-plasma technologies.

Dual-use technology is technology for military and civil purposes. From the point of view of resource efficiency, it is necessary to provide the best military technologies to the civilian sector of the economy. This is the essence of dual-use technologies, which determines their value. It is advisable for the military-technical complex to share its production capacity with the civilian sector in order to produce the latest consumer goods and services on an industrial scale.

Dual-use technology emerged after the Second World War, when it was time to convert not only a huge number of weapons, but also to turn military factories into factories that can meet the rapidly growing demand of the population for consumer goods. Dual-use technologies have introduced products such as computers, telecommunications equipment for offices, medical instruments, calibration and measurement devices, including electronic-optical surveillance and detection systems, as well as environmental monitoring technologies, and much more to the civilian sector.

It is possible to distinguish the following main directions of scientific and technical activity of the state on creation of breakthrough technologies of dual-use: laser, nuclear-thermonuclear, plasmophysical, rocket and space, optoelectronic, microwave, information, communication, non-traditional energy, construction, medical and preventive, training, diagnostic, agro-industrial.

Аmong the breakthrough technologies, we can distinguish the category of so-called closing technologies. Their appearance and use makes a huge number of existing technologies unnecessary and outdated. For example, the advent of the computer made unnecessary the flourishing industries in the past associated with the production of arithmetic meters, logarithmic rulers and other similar products that were widely used in economic practice. closing energy-and resource-saving breakthrough technologies can make revolutionary changes in the country's economy.

Modern breakthrough technologies are the product of collective creativity and, as a rule, require high-class specialists for their implementation. therefore, the state is called upon to provide society with highly qualified personnel, increasing investment in education sphere.
