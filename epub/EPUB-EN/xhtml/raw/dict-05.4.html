  • Technology park management
    is the function of state authorities and local authorities. The government develops large-scale programs to support small and medium business, provides it with legislative assistance, motivates the development of innovative technologies in various ways. The local government creates conditions conducive to attracting innovation projects of specialist innovators to work on innovative projects in order to achieve the creation of small innovative knowledge-intensive firms in their technology parks. The problems they solve can refer to any aspect of management activity.
  • Specialization of innovative managers
    initiation of new ideas at all stages, beginning with a stage of research and development and up to creation and realization of innovations; maintenance of communication communications by accumulation of the scientific and technical and market information necessary for functioning of the organization (by means of incorporation of knowledge and their distribution in the organization); Creation of conditions for perception and advancement of new ideas and orientation of participants of innovative process on the final result; leadership in advancement of innovations, including in the sphere of new forms of the personnel work organization (adequate to innovative activity).
  • Requirements to managers of innovative projects:
    high level of professionalism and the qualification that guarantee efficiency of activity; ability to carry out business designing, forecasting and planning of innovative processes, carrying out of the economic analysis, calculation of risks, search of possibilities to expand spheres for organization activity.
