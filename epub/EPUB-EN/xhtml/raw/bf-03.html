Innovation policy and innovation activity

Keywords

  • An innovation process is a way to transform scientific knowledge (ideas) into a concrete result (innovation of one kind or another).
  • State policy in the field of science and technology is to implement the most important innovation processes, if possible focused on the creation of breakthrough technologies of national significance.
  • State regulation of innovation activities is carried out in order to ensure progressive changes in the sphere of material production and increase the competitiveness of the national product by strengthening the position of domestic producers in the world markets of goods and services.
