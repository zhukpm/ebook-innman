  • Competitive advantage
    added value for the customer in an innovative product. It is achieved on the basis of the aggressive market strategy, focused on superiority in innovative activities over competitors. Factors of competitiveness of the organization are changeable and do not allow to keep competitive advantages which are connected with realization of the increasing changes in innovative strategies, products and technologies. Long-term competitive advantage is a hard to produce value, or a set of interrelated values inherent in an innovative product that require significant time, spiritual strength, intellectual and financial resources. Competitive advantage is a universal indicator that characterizes innovation.
