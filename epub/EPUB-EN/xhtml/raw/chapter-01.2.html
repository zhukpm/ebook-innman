The main social function of the production and economic sphere is to meet the material needs of the population (if possible in an innovative way). Without interaction with the authorities, designed to create conditions for this, and certain relations with the society, for the sake of which this sphere functions, its activities are impossible. The function of satisfying material needs is performed by the subjects of production and economic activity (i.e. enterprises of various forms of ownership and management, and personally - entrepreneurs who act as carriers of innovative ideas).3.

In a market economy, this area is represented by business structures and the business community as a whole. As an explicit function of the entrepreneur (entrepreneur-private owner), as well as individual, and to some extent associated (joint-stock, cooperative, collective), the function of profit maximization acts. However, as Adam Smith observed, it is not the generosity of the butcher, brewer, or Baker that we count on when we look forward to our dinner, but the fact that he, pursuing his own special interest and guided by an invisible hand, achieves results that were not even his intention. This result is a satisfied effective demand, as a hidden social function of the entrepreneur.

An entrepreneur can maximize profit only by reinvesting a certain part of it in the expanded reproduction of individual (private) capital, which in turn is an element of public national capital. Thus, the effective implementation of main function of business (profit maximization) coupled in production growth of the entire social capital in its money form, increasing of production volume of gross social product. This means that the main function of profit maximization is coupled with the hidden social function of expanded reproduction of social capital through expanded reproduction of individual (private) capital. Profit maximization is also achieved by optimal combination of such basic factors of production (means used in the production of goods) as land, labor and capital, the functioning of which requires special technologies and mechanisms of interaction with the government, business and society and taking into account not only the purely economic, but also the social interests of the participants in this process.

Recently, another specific factor of modern production has been singled out — information. Information is an ordered system of knowledge and dates used in economic processes that requires time and other resources to search for and obtain. With the development of the scientific and technological revolution, the flow of information and its role in the economy has increased significantly, which has made it possible to use information as the most important object of innovation management in the context of digitalization, the development of machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

In order to maximize profit, the mentioned production factors should be connected and interact in the most effective way, i.e. their optimal combination should be achieved on an innovative basis (selection and implementation of new technical, technological and organizational solutions in the production process, as well as taking into account the social consequences of economic growth). Due to these circumstances, entrepreneurial ability acts as a resource of a special kind. Entrepreneurial abilities are specific abilities of a person that allow them to find and use the best combination of resources in the production process, create and apply novelties, take reasonable risks, while preserving and improving the human habitation environment. To do this, the entrepreneur must be the owner in the full sense of the word, caring not only for the well-being of his own business, but also for the preservation of the habitation environment in which his farm operates, including nature, lifestyle, traditions and culture.

The result is the optimum combination of implementation of the main private function of business (maximize profits by reducing costs) and rational use of labor, natural and intellectual resources, the transition to innovative type of economy development and its new quality of growth in saved surrounded by the culture of the peoplehood.

It is the presence of these hidden social functions that makes the entrepreneur figure dominant in the market economy, which is very effective in comparison with other well-known models of organizing the economic life activity of society. The entrepreneur differs from a simple robber in that his desire to increase his own capital is directly associated with the growth of public capital in its natural-material and value dimensions. The state as part of the implementation of its basic function (ensuring a balance of interests in society) it is intended to assist entrepreneurs in performing hidden social functions in every possible way, and to prevent them from being suppressed by main functions of private nature. At the same time, it is very important to skillfully build relations between business, the government and society on the basis of mutual dependence in order to achieve a balance of interests in the most rational and innovative way.
