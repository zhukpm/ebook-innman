  • Motivation:
    is a complex and dynamic process of inducing an employee (with the help of intrapersonal and external factors) to purposeful activity aimed at achieving innovative individual and organizational goals (setting and encouraging specific objectives). It promotes such behavior of the employees, which allows to reach the planned objectives through successful work in a team.
  • Motives and incentives.
    Stimulus - an external reason, capable to cause in the worker some motives to the activity realized by him/her. Motive - internal stimulus that activates directed human activity.
  • Creative work
    the work of individuals with the capacity for analytical thinking, a heightened sense of self-worth and autonomy in action. The success of the innovation process increasingly depends on their commitment.
