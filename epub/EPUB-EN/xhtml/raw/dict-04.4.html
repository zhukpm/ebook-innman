  • Leader of the new generation
    a highly spiritual person with a new way of thinking, innovative management culture, ethical values.
  • Principles of leadership, ensuring success
    the ability to learn from own mistakes, to develop the ability to think comprehensively and strategically, to be able to risk, learn new things, clearly assess their strengths; perceive other ways of thinking and behavior, different from their own, the ability to change and adapt, mobilize and organize people, taking into account existing standards and views.
  • Components of leadership:
    the ability to focus attention and resources on obtaining a high result; disclosure of the natural source of energy within each employee; the leader's ability to ignite enthusiasm; rapid establishment of connections between people; support of initiatives and encouragement of innovative activity; selection of the right people and their placement according to who and what is capable; mastery of the art of performing the function of "social architect", including conflict resolution; removal of various obstacles to the application of creative forces.
  • Features of an effective innovative management team:
    consideration of the characteristics and qualifications of each team member; complementarity of skills and the ability to perform various role functions; understanding and acceptance of tasks by all team members; interest in realizing their creative potential; developing alternative solutions and choosing the most acceptable of them by common consent; complementarity, finding ways to resolve emerging problems; respect for the opinions of colleagues; narrowing the scope of distribution of formal relations.
