  • Innovative project
    the plan to resolve a problem on the basis of the system of the interconnected purposes focused on the change of the innovative organization. Components of system of the purposes are interconnected on resources, terms and executors who solve various research, development, financial and organizational problems of the innovative project. As the purpose of the project can be an organization gaining a leading position in its industry and creation of competitive advantages.
  • The program managing method
    the method based on programs and projects. It assumes the use of adequate methods of purposeful influence on objects, developed in the concepts of programs. Programs are the object of interest of the budget policy of the organization: through them the most important tasks of the development of the country are implemented. Federal, presidential, regional, sectoral and object-target programs are distinguished by their significance. Federal target programs are designed to solve the most acute problems, constraining the development of society. By content, there are scientific and technical, social, economic, investment, defense and other programs. All programs are designed to have an active impact on the progress of corresponding spheres of national economy and implement the common national interests.
  • Project management
    the name of an independent multidisciplinary discipline that combines special and supraprofessional knowledge. Project management means art of coordination of actions of executors and management of all kinds of purposefully used resources. It involves: justification of the main innovative idea; formation of the general goal; achievement of results in terms of timing, size of profit, as well as the volume of investments in terms of their forms and sources; development of the innovative project and plan for its implementation; creation of a special organization (project team); delivery of the results to the customer and closing of contracts.
