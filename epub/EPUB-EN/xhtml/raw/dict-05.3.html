Technology parks. Small business incubators

  • Incubator
    one of the necessary structural departments of modern technology parks, designed to solve the problem of business organization project substantiation and its further successful functioning. The innovative incubator is the basis of technology park. Commercialization of research and development results takes place in it, which are carried out on preferential terms in small innovative firms. Incubators are located in technology development zones, in specially selected areas, where all conditions for the development of innovative activity are created.
  • Small innovative firms
    the companies having certain advantages in comparison with large companies in realization of innovative functions due to their immanent properties: initiative and solidarity of the personnel, entrepreneurial spirit, readiness to take a certain risk, quick reaction to changes of the environment of functioning.
  • Technology park
    (or technology incubator) is a scientific and production territorial complex with a complex functional structure, designed to form the most favorable environment for development of small science-intensive firms. The structure of a technology park is characterized by a set of small but various firms - centers specialized in providing certain innovative services, which in their turn are a part of technopolis (research, innovative (scientific and technical complex), marketing, training center), and an industrial area. It is based on modular principle, the primary element of which is a small formation - an innovative firm-incubator.
  • Technopolis
    scientific-industrial structure, in which a significant place is occupied by technology parks. General target functions of technopolises are: formation of innovation infrastructure and increase of innovation activity; structural reorganization of industry; creation of new jobs; development of mechanisms of innovation activity; increase of innovation ability and increase of innovation potential and scientific intensity of industry; improvement of state innovation policy. The main motivation is making profit by all participants.
  • Science-introduction zone
    set of technopolises, each of which is represented by a number of technology parks, having in its composition a certain number of innovative incubators. In this zone should certainly be created common for all branched out infrastructure, supporting their innovative and industrial activity.
