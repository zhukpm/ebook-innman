Sources and forms of innovations financing

Keywords

  • Venture capital (venture risk entrepreneurship) is a source of financing for young fast-growing industries designed to develop and bring a scientific idea (invention) to the stage of practical use, up to the formation of demand for innovative scientific and technical products.
  • Financial leasing is a type of operation financing that is used for rapid industrial development of major innovations. A type of business activity oriented investment of temporarily free or involved financial resources, when the leasing contract (financial lease) tenant agrees to acquire ownership of the contracted property from the certain seller and to grant this property available to the lessor for payment for temporary use.
  • Intellectual property is a significant asset of an innovative organization that serves as an effective weapon in competition. For smart intellectual property management, it is particularly important that the personnel involved in this process (authors and owners of rights to the technology, investors and entrepreneurs) are well aware of the conditions for its commercialization.
