  • The project life cycle
    includes all stages of innovative activity associated with the transformation of scientific and technological ideas into a new or improved product or technological process, or a new approach to social services.
  • The phases of the life cycle of the project:
    the investor's idea, analysis, concept development (determining the final objectives and ways to achieve them); project development, project implementation (planning a structurally defined sequence of stages of work leading to a set of goals established by the interested parties; detailed working design and delivery, construction), operation, elimination.
