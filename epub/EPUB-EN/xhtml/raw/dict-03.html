Innovation policy and innovation activity

  1. Goals, tasks, process
  2. Innovation activity
  3. Types and ways of competitive advantages forming
Topic summary

Innovation policy is an integral part of the state socio-economic policy. It represents the attitude of state authorities to innovation activity and its results. It is a kind of a "beacon" for innovative activity on the scale of national economy and a strategic reference point for business in terms of building development goals and concentration of resources necessary for their achievement. The innovative policy is a powerful regulator of innovative activity, promotes creation of favorable innovative climate for maintenance of the state priorities of scientific and technical development in economic sphere. Innovation activity is an innovative process that transforms scientific and technological knowledge into innovation. It is focused on creation of competitive advantages - values for the buyer, put in an innovative product, and allows the organization to be a leader on the market. The achievement of such a result becomes possible only by using the corresponding innovation management system.
