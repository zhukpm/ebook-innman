Innovation culture

Keywords

  • Corporate culture is a model of constructive interaction between equal partners of an innovation organization. The resulting communication environment contributes to the expansion of the space of creativity and the creation of conditions for self-realization of partners in professional activities on the basis of corporate relations.
  • Ethics is the most important component of corporate culture, inseparable from a person's thinking, and invisibly regulating his actions, making them more effective. The science of morality recognizes ethics as designed to solve practical moral problems that arise in front of a person.
  • Justice is a universal motive of human behavior, which is considered one of the most important principles of social system management, which have a huge motivational potential.
