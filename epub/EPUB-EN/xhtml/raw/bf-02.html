Innovations and innovation potential

Keywords

  • Innovation refers to the final result of innovation activity that is implemented on the market in the form of a new or improved product (technological process).
  • Scientific staff is represented by creative workers engaged in scientific research and development which increasing the amount of knowledge and new areas of their application.
  • The concept of scientific and technological development is a document developed at all levels of management structures of the innovative economy and based on the possibilities of new knowledge and intellectual labor.
