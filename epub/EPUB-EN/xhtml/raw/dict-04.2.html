Goals and principles

  • The classification criteria of innovative goals:
    level of decision-making (strategic and tactical - branch and enterprise); type of environment in relation to the organization (internal and external); content (economic, scientific, technical, organizational, etc.). Functions (production, research, development, finance, marketing, personnel); stages of the life cycle of the organization (project, program) - emergence, growth, maturity, decline and completion of the life cycle.
  • Priority innovative purposes of the organization
    (from the position of internal needs); strengthening of competitive advantages; increase of production efficiency; improvement of the system of services rendered to consumers on the basis of scientific and technical, organizational, intellectual and economic potential of the organization; increase of wages (remuneration) of employees; improvement of working conditions; increase of social security of the personnel.
  • Priority, structural and tactical goals of the organization
    objectives focused on growth and development of the organization through the activization of innovative activity, as well as the promotion of new products and technologies on the market. Structural objectives are the optimization of activity of profile interfaced subsystems of organization - production, scientific and technical services, finances, personnel. Tactical purposes are considered to be the purposes that are favorable to intensification of the processes of development, mastering and implementation of innovations, training of the corresponding staff, stimulation of their activity (taking into account the peculiarities of motivation).
  • The factors that influence on the development of innovative organization.
    External - causing changes in the organization: the culture and ethical values of the organization; political stability; the law; policy documents of government regulation; suppliers; consumers; competitors; intermediaries; economy, ecology, living standards of the population. Internal - influencing directly on innovation of the organization: staff qualification, knowledge-intensiveness of production; type of production and its technological level; diversification of production; the quality of products; duration of the product circulation cycle.
  • Innovative purpose:
    creation of innovation, realized in the given terms at available (as a rule, limited) resources, for essential improvement of quality of development of innovation object (solution of corresponding problem situation). The innovative purposes supposing development of principle innovations, can be connected with carrying out of works on patenting and licensing, getting of "know-how" and industrial samples, a life cycle of innovation. Innovative purposes cover all structure of management.
