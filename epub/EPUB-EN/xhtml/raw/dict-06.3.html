  • Spheres of the organization behavior strategy development:
    new markets and efforts concentration on a limited segment of the market; specialization in manufacturing products or technology; development of new products and services; improving services for consumers; assistance in improving product quality; increasing capitalization level; increasing return on investments and profit; achieving leadership in a certain area of innovative activity.
  • Types of innovative strategies:
    offensive - innovation, aimed at conquering the market; imitation - acquisition of new technologies (products) under license; traditional - improving the quality of products; opportunistic - development of a strategic product at relatively low cost of research and development; defensive - not allowing lagging behind competitors in technological development.
