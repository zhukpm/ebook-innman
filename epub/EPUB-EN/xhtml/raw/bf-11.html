Breakthrough innovation technologies

Keywords

  • Breakthrough innovations. Breakthrough are considered innovations (product, service, or change in development strategy) that lead to a significant increase in the organization's revenue and net profit.
  • Breakthrough technology is such a concentration of energy of various knowledge that, under certain conditions, "break" the usual (traditional) ideas about an object, opening up space for fundamentally new opportunities for its qualitative transformation.
  • Basic source of breakthrough technologies is culture, the values that flow from it and the accumulated knowledge of humanity, which allow the state to create special conditions that contribute to the formation and implementation of breakthrough technologies.
  • Formation of breakthrough technologies is a special type of activity of the state and business, focused on meeting the needs of the real sector of the economy. Large-scale and effective development of breakthrough technologies is possible if this task is the basis of the country's state policy, which provides large-scale support to all participants in this process.
  • Nanotechnology is a modern tool for creating artifacts that act as special objects of innovation management. Nanotechnologies allow us to create a qualitatively new state of matter, new materials with specified properties.
