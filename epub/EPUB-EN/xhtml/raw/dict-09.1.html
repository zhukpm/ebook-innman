  • Innovative culture
    a set of innovative achievements of society based on the traditions of the Russian people, reflecting their desire for education, knowledge, development of non-standard thinking, use of natural intuitive abilities, creativity and teamwork, as well as such qualities of the Russian people as receptivity to innovation, tolerance to others and different worldviews. Skillful use of these traditions and qualities of people in innovative activity provides outstripping growth of spiritual and intellectual capital of organization, promotes an active increase in the number of innovations and their competitiveness. In this case formed a new quality of the results of innovative activity of the organization, which dominates the social, spiritual over material, ensuring the sustainability of its functioning.
  • The pyramid of corporate responsibility:
    economic responsibility - to be profitable and to pay taxes honestly (the basis of all other types of responsibility); legal responsibility - to comply with the letter of the law (playing by the same rules); philanthropic responsibility; corporate social responsibility of government, business and society (ability to improve quality of life).
  • Ethics
    the most important component of corporate culture, based on the spiritual values of the people, invisibly regulating their actions, making them more effective. As a science of morality, ethics is recognized as a means of solving practical moral problems that arise before a person. Ethical norms of business relations become one of the most important components of innovative activity of each employee and the organization as a whole. They contribute to an organization’s corporate culture based on values such as love, joy, peace and patience, because "there is no law on such things".
