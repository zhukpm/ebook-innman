  • Innovations in business
    a way of creation of new sources of profit or expansion of existing ones, the final result of innovative activity, realized in the market in the form of a new or improved product (technological process), as a result of which application in production there are fundamental changes that lead to cardinal organizational, administrative and industrial-technological transformations. With the help of innovations, organizations implement modern methods and technologies of production.
  • Innovation activity in the state sphere and business
    work of the personnel on creation, development, distribution and use of political, legal, personnel, social, financial, economic, organizational, administrative, technological and other innovations connected with interaction and functioning of authority, business and society. It includes a complex of scientific, organizational, financial measures on accumulation and use of knowledge directed on revealing or creation, distribution of innovations. The results of innovation activities are public services with improved qualities, advanced technologies and indirect results in the activities of public authorities.
  • The innovative policy in the state sphere and business

    activity of the state, public organizations and business, directed on creation of conditions for achievement of balance of interests in system of interrelation and functioning of authority, business and society by innovative way.

    It becomes the most important instrument to create long-term competitive advantages, to keep national guidelines of life activities, to ensure sustainability and stability of development. The strategy of state development and improvement of the quality of life of the population is closely connected with it.

  • Innovation sphere of economy
    sphere of priority dissemination of innovations. It is represented by the whole range of global companies, large branches of national economy, medium and small business, state and municipal administration. The source of innovation is novation, which is used both directly in production and management and is adequate to the quality of the changing system of regulated market relations.
  • Innovation process in the system of interaction of the state sphere and business
    a way of transformation of scientific knowledge (idea) into concrete result (innovation of this or that kind). It is a process of creation, development, distribution and use of innovation (political, legal, personnel, social, financial, economic, organizational, managerial, technological). It is carried out through consistent transformation of an innovative idea into a real product that increases the efficiency of interaction and its useful results for the government, business and society. The innovative process covers the cycle of operations from the idea's emergence to its practical implementation.
  • Innovations in system of interaction of the state sphere and business
    the complete innovative process of qualitative modification of the relations inherent in this or that sphere, characterized by long-term effect as a result of which cardinal changes in the joint activity take place, improving possibilities of everyone and results of life activity of a society.
  • The scientific personnel of the organization
    creative employees involved in scientific research and development, increasing the effect of knowledge and new areas of their application.
  • Innovation
    the process of creation, distribution and use in economic and other systems of innovations that have (in comparison with existing analogues) the ability to provide more effective work of the organization, focused on the adoption of certain needs. Innovation is used in any sphere as an improvement that allows to achieve cost savings or to create the appropriate conditions for it.
  • The results obtained from the use of innovations
    are numerous and diverse: market renewal; expansion of the range of goods and services; creation of new methods and technologies of production, supply and sale; increase in the efficiency of management and competitiveness of products; profit growth; improvement of social protection of the population; increase in the level of resource saving; reduction of material, energy and capital intensity of products; rapid expansion of new technological systems that ensure environmental cleanliness.
