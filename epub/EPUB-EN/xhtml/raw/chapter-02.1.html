Innovations and innovation potential

1. Entering the innovation economy

In the life activity of most states, a period of transition to an innovative stage of development is coming. An increasing number of factors are beginning to appear in the economic system that indicate that states are entering a qualitatively new innovative environment of social, industrial and economic relations. This is a very important moment in the development of states, since they usually need not only to update the production base, but also to introduce modern innovative technologies into the management system of various organizations.

The level of innovative development of the state increases the ability to effectively solve not only internal problems, but also to achieve external goals. For their implementation, each specific country must occupy an appropriate place in the world economic system, worthy of its level of development.

In the era of large-scale changes, modern innovation policy is becoming a most important tool for creating long-term competitive advantages, preserving national guidelines, and ensuring sustainability and stability of development. It is no accident that innovation activity is increasingly linked to the prospects for the development of states and improving the quality of life of the world's population.

The modern stage of civilization development is characterized by rapid changes of the geopolitical and economic space. New goals and objectives for the functioning of economic structures are constantly being put forward, obliging staff to respond quickly to changes and take appropriate for changes measures within the organization, make significant adjustments to the management system. deglobalization processes are intensifying, competition is becoming more intense and tougher, the product life cycle is shortening, the demand for research and development and new forms of cooperation is growing. The need to make innovative decisions and use effective forms of adaptation of organizations to the constantly changing real and virtual life activity environment dominates.

The growth in the number of scientific achievements, human capital, production potential and productive power of labor, scientific and technological progress have an increasing impact on the economy of organizations. Scientific research and digital technological developments expand the prospects for using existing technical capabilities, improving the efficiency of production and management.

Innovation problematics are becoming more and more relevant. It is innovations that lead to the renewal of the market, the expansion of the product and service nomenclature, the creation of new production and logistics methods, and the improvement of management efficiency and product competitiveness.

In Russia, the innovative sphere of the economy is represented by a set of branches of the national economy and spheres of life of the population. The source of its development is innovations that are adequate to the quality of the changing system of regulated market relations, used both directly in production and in management.

The most important condition for the development of the innovation sphere is the national innovation system. Building a national innovation system in a country begins with developing a concept and making appropriate decisions at the political level, according to which intellectual and financial resources are concentrated, and innovative organizations are rapidly developing. The national innovation system involves the implementation of a process, starting from the production of knowledge and ending with its implementation in innovative goods, services and technologies.

The main subjects of the innovation process are the primary links of the economic system — real and virtual enterprises, organizations, whose development prospects are largely determined by the effectiveness of innovations. The innovative activity of an enterprise or organization is conditioned by the objective need for the development of science and technology, the laws of market relations associated with the orientation of economic entities to demand and increasing market needs, increased competition, and more complex economic relations.

The competitive advantages and competitiveness of knowledge-based organizations are provided by intellectual resources, corporate, general and digital culture, innovative technologies, the level of training of personnel, their ability to develop and implement competitive strategies.

Russia is at the stage of reforming and forming an innovation system, the quality of which is adequate to the requirements of the market. Russian scientists and practitioners are making great and increasing efforts to achieve this goal. There is achieved a common understanding of the need to develop an innovation economy based on knowledge which embodied in intellectual work and appropriate for it relations. The country is faced with the task of intellectualizing labor, changing labor relations on this basis, accompanied by radical changes in the entire system of organizations' life activity.

The processes of formation of an innovation economy indicate that the essence of the problem is not so much in determining who develops innovation policy (which, of course, is important in itself), but in who and on the basis of what inter-relations it is implemented.

For the formation of an innovation economy, the development goal sets of which are focused on universal values, it is necessary to change the person himself and not only the individual or the team, but the entire generation. We need highly spiritual people who profess the philosophy of innovation management with a proper understanding, who have not only sufficient competence, qualifications, communication skills and leadership qualities, but also high culture in the broadest sense of the word.

States that rely on such people will have long-term competitive advantages that can ensure their leadership in creating breakthrough technologies that preserve the traditions and culture of a multinational people and will take their rightful place in a globalizing world with its innovation economy.
