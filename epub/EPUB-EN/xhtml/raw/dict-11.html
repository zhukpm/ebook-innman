Topic summary

Breakthrough technologies are one of the results of innovation management applications in government and business. The basic source of breakthrough technologies is culture. Values arising from it and accumulated knowledge of mankind allow the state to create the special conditions promoting formation and implementation of breakthrough technologies. The major condition is a high concentration of intellectual potential of experts in various spheres of science and production in priority directions of development.
