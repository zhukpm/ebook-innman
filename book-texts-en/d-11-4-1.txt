Nanotechnology. manipulating objects in the size of one billionth of a meter (nanometer, the size of an atom). The modern tool of creation of the artifacts acting as special objects of innovative management. Nanotechnology allows the creation of a qualitatively new state of matter, new materials with predetermined properties. Promises mankind a controllable construction of a new matter. It has wide prospects of application in various areas of science and industry, including production of new types of materials, telecommunication systems, energy sources and methods of treatment.
