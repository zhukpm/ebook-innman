Innovation activity. the activity based on creation, development, dissemination and application of scientific and technical knowledge in all areas of science and technology. As a result, productive forces and organizational-economical relations in economic systems are transformed. Innovation activity itself is an innovative process - the process of transformation of scientific and technical knowledge into innovations when in a chain of events starting with a new idea, it is matured, embodied in a concrete product, technology or service form and further spreads.
