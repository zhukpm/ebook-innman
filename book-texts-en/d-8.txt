Topic summary.

Innovation activities are motivated by intrapersonal and external factors. Motivation ensures such behavior of the employees, which allows achieving the set goals through successful work in the team. The sources of motivation are: freedom of choice, the right to decide and independence. The serious motives of innovative organization are profitability; creativity; satisfaction of consumers' demands, formation of new needs; prestige.
The diversity of mutually reinforcing motivations should be oriented towards specific innovations, the bearers of which are the creative personnel.
