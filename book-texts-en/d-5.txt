Topic summary.

Organizational forms of innovation management are represented by a variety of specialized business structures or their departments. The variety of organizational forms of innovative management and structural forms of innovations is caused by variety of objects of innovations. The most widespread organizational forms of small innovative business are: leasing, franchising, engineering, venture financing, incubator, technology park, scientific innovative zone, technopolis. The variety of organizational forms of small innovative business provides wide possibilities for creation and distribution of each specialized innovation.
Organizational forms of innovation management are closely connected with innovation projects, which are assignment by plans based on the system of interconnected purposes, aimed at changing of the innovation organization.
