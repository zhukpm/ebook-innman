Indirect regulation of the innovation activities of organizations. is carried out without direct government involvement. The development of science and technology and the mastering of their achievements in the public and private sectors of the economy is regulated by tax, depreciation, patent and customs policies, as well as by supporting innovative small enterprises, providing various benefits to the subjects of the innovation process; creating an innovation climate in the economy, the development of infrastructure for research and development, including services for the preparation of forecasts of scientific and technological development and the formation of a system of national scientific priorities on their basis.
