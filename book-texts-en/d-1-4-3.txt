The public-power sphere. includes the legislature, executive and judicial branches of state power, their organization in regions - subjects of the Federation, as well as legislative and executive bodies of local self-government. In other words, the public-power sphere is personified in the state bodies, municipal government.
It is designed to guarantee the balance of interests of the subjects of industrial-economics, socio-political and public-power activities, as well as to provide general conditions for effective functionalization and development of all major spheres of society.
