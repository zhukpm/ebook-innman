General, basic functions of the state. providing the balance of economic interests of subjects of economic and socio-political life of the company; providing the conditions of life of the company as a whole and the industrial-economics sphere in particular.
In modern conditions, these functions are concretized in the following specific functions: ensuring the legal framework and the social atmosphere necessary for the effective functioning of the market economy; protection of competition; support of socially fair distribution of incomes; adjustment of the distribution of resources in order to change the structure of the national product; stabilization of the economy, i.e. control of the employment rate and inflation, generated by economic fluctuations; stimulation of economic growth.
