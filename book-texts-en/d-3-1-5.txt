Federal innovation policy. is to create a favorable innovation climate for the implementation of state priorities of scientific and technical development in the economic sphere. At the national level, the innovation policy includes: initiation of innovation programs and projects aimed at the implementation of scientific and technical innovations; transfer of new technologies from the public sector to the real sector of economy; creation of the innovation market and its infrastructure; conducting research and development in accordance with the system of state scientific priorities; legal regulation of the state patent policy and standardization policy.
