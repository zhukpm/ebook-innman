Basic source of innovation in the context of globalization. Culture is the basic source of innovation. Innovation is drawn from culture, but culture also changes under its influence. Strategies to build organizations are influenced by culture. American, British, Japanese and Chinese organizations have their own ways of shaping strategies based on the characteristics of national cultures and their role in realizing their goals. In some cases, the formation of multinational organizations is dominated by the culture of their country of origin; in others, the corporate culture introduced by migration essentially coincides with the national culture; and in a third case, cultures are fused on the basis of universally recognized, universal values.
