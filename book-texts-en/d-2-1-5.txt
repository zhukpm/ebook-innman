Innovation process in the system of interaction of the state sphere and business. a way of transformation of scientific knowledge (idea) into concrete result (innovation of this or that kind). It is a process of creation, development, distribution and use of innovation (political, legal, personnel, social, financial, economic, organizational, managerial, technological). It is carried out through consistent transformation of an innovative idea into a real product that increases the efficiency of interaction and its useful results for the government, business and society. The innovative process covers the cycle of operations from the idea's emergence to its practical implementation.
