Criteria and indicators for evaluation and selection of innovative projects. Financial and economic criteria and corresponding indicators are mainly used. If necessary, when assessing qualitative parameters, characteristics are analyzed, as a rule, not having a quantitative assessment, but significantly supplementing the ideas about the activity of innovative organization. In addition to financial and economic criteria, there are normative criteria, criteria of resource provision, and strategic criteria. As indicators there are: the cost of the project; profitability, calculated on the basis of capital investments; payback period; efficiency factor; savings on conditionally constant expenses and others.
