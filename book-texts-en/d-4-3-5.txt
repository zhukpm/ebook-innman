Planning function. the most important function of management, which determines specific, reasoned goals and objectives, develops methods and means of their implementation. The plan and the forecast are mutually complementary stages of management (with the defining role of the plan). Unlike the forecast, the plan contains not alternative, but unequivocal characteristics of the planned object, based on rational forecast variant. The plan serves as a basis of innovative organizations activity, providing coordinated work of their subdivisions and determination of need in resources.
