Content of innovation management. study of processes of the innovation creation; development of plans (programs) of innovation activity; coordination of activity of all production subdivisions of the organization from the point of view of the system approach; provision of projects (programs) of innovation activity with financial and material resources; equipment of innovation subdivisions with qualified personnel and formation of target groups in them, as a rule, temporary for realization of innovation problems; control over the course of creation of a new program of innovation activity.
