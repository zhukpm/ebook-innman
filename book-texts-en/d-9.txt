Topic summary.

Innovation culture is a basic resource of an organization. It is closely connected to the corporate culture providing effective interaction of partners of the innovative organization. The major role in development of innovative and corporate culture is played by a manager if creativity is supported, is able to motivate initiators and conductors of resultant changes, discusses with the persona of the organization the sense and benefit of changes, provides other conditions necessary for getting high innovative results. The innovative culture to some extent underlies all innovative administrative decisions.
