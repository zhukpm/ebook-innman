Functions of the state. the basic directions of activity of the state, allowing it to play a relevant role in society.
In the advanced civil society, the functions of the state are concentrated on: formulation of the general strategy of development; identification and substantiation of priorities, rates, proportions of the growth of economic and social spheres of society; stimulation of socially useful activity of citizens; protection of their rights, property and personal dignity; democratization of all spheres of life of people; protection of borders and provision of necessary internal order.
