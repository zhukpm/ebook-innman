Principles of innovative activity. priority that supposes necessity of inclusion in the plan of innovations, perspective of which is confirmed by forecasts, calculations and which realization provides considerable economic and social benefits not only in short-term, but also in medium-term and long-term periods; continuity that obliges to consecutive working out short-term, medium-term and long-term plans of innovative development; systematicity - through planning on all stages of life cycle of innovation subject, especially in large organizations, and Complexity - close interrelation of the innovative plan with adequate sections of the plan of economic and social development of the organization, the industrial program, investment and financial plans, personnel policy of the organization; economic substantiation - inclusion in the plan of only economically substantiated actions provided with necessary resources presented in the business plan of the organization.
