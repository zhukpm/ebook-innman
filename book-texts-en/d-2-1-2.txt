Innovation activity in the state sphere and business. work of the personnel on creation, development, distribution and use of political, legal, personnel, social, financial, economic, organizational, administrative, technological and other innovations connected with interaction and functioning of authority, business and society. It includes a complex of scientific, organizational, financial measures on accumulation and use of knowledge directed on revealing or creation, distribution of innovations. The results of innovation activities are public services with improved qualities, advanced technologies and indirect results in the activities of public authorities.
