Project management. the name of an independent multidisciplinary discipline that combines special and supraprofessional knowledge. Project management means art of coordination of actions of executors and management of all kinds of purposefully used resources. It involves: justification of the main innovative idea; formation of the general goal; achievement of results in terms of timing, size of profit, as well as the volume of investments in terms of their forms and sources; development of the innovative project and plan for its implementation; creation of a special organization (project team); delivery of the results to the customer and closing of contracts.
