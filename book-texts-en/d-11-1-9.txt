Breakthrough technology. a concentration of diverse knowledge energy that, under certain conditions, 'explodes' the conventional (traditional) understanding of an object, opening up space for fundamentally new possibilities of its qualitative transformation. It allows an organization to achieve impressive results in a short period of time, and to be significantly ahead of its competitors. It is a product of national innovation system and innovation management, an important factor of economic growth and social development. It includes a set of production processes, scientific description of production methods or management methods that have no analogues in the world and lead to revolutionary changes in various spheres of human life.
