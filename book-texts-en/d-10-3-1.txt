Methods of working up group solutions of innovative organization development. research methods aimed at finding a practical solution to the problem of innovative organization: "Brainstorming" method (each participant of "brainstorming" contributes to the solution of the problem); expert method (evaluation of solutions is carried out by invited expert specialists, who combine the knowledge of the specifics of the examined object with knowledge in the field of management science); method of scenarios (consecutive events that bring the problem situation to the desired finite state with a certain probability are described in detail); Risk solutions (estimated the level of probability of achieving the results, failures and deviations from the predicted objectives through quantitative and qualitative risk analysis); intuitive decisions (based on intuition, which have managers who can foresee the future of the organization).
