The program managing method. the method based on programs and projects. It assumes the use of adequate methods of purposeful influence on objects, developed in the concepts of programs. Programs are the object of interest of the budget policy of the organization: through them the most important tasks of the development of the country are implemented. Federal, presidential, regional, sectoral and object-target programs are distinguished by their significance. Federal target programs are designed to solve the most acute problems, constraining the development of society. By content, there are scientific and technical, social, economic, investment, defense and other programs. All programs are designed to have an active impact on the progress of corresponding spheres of national economy and implement the common national interests.
