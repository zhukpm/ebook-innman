Incubator. one of the necessary structural departments of modern technology parks, designed to solve the problem of business organization project substantiation and its further successful functioning. The innovative incubator is the basis of technology park. Commercialization of research and development results takes place in it, which are carried out on preferential terms in small innovative firms. Incubators are located in technology development zones, in specially selected areas, where all conditions for the development of innovative activity are created.
