Priority, structural and tactical goals of the organization. objectives focused on growth and development of the organization through the activization of innovative activity, as well as the promotion of new products and technologies on the market. Structural objectives are the optimization of activity of profile interfaced subsystems of organization - production, scientific and technical services, finances, personnel. Tactical purposes are considered to be the purposes that are favorable to intensification of the processes of development, mastering and implementation of innovations, training of the corresponding staff, stimulation of their activity (taking into account the peculiarities of motivation).
