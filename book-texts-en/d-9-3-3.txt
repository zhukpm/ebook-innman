Requirements for the manager of an innovative organization. openness and commitment to innovative ideas; support for creativity; facilitate adaptation to changing conditions; ensure that the organization’s strategy is aligned with the long-term interests of all those affected by it (customers, suppliers, employees, shareholders, etc.); ability to motivate the initiators and agents of effective change for the future; awareness of the fit between strategy and culture; communicating with the organization’s staff about the meaning and usefulness of the changes being implemented (not only their appropriateness but also their possibility).
