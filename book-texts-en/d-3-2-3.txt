Innovation process. a process that is focused on the creation of a new product, technology (discovery, invention and in general "know-how"), or activities, the final result of which is the commercial implementation of a new product (technology, product, materials), technology, including new methods of organization and production management. The innovation process does not end with the introduction (appearance on the market) of a new product and technology. As the innovation spreads, it continues to improve, discover previously unknown properties and becomes more and more effective.
