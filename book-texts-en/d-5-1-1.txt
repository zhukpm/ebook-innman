Organizational forms of innovative management. specialized business organizations or the divisions various for the reason of an extreme variety of objects (processes) of innovations. They include: central services on mastering of new production; project-target groups on carrying out of scientific researches, development and manufacturing of production; departments of research and development; venture departments; specialized centralized funds of stimulation of innovations; consulting and analytical groups; temporary target groups for complex decision of innovative problems; venture groups; engineering firms.
