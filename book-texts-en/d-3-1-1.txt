State innovation policy in science and technology. is the implementation of major innovation processes aimed at creating breakthrough technologies of state importance. It is an integral part of the state's social and economic policy, which presents its vision, ideology, goals and objectives for innovation. It is focused on solving innovative problems of intersectoral and sectoral nature that lead to a radical change in the country's technological base and require concentration of resources on the national economy scale. It expresses the attitude of the state authorities to innovation activity and its results.
