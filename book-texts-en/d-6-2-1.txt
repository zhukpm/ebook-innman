Innovation forecasting. assessment and choice of acceptable managerial decisions among several variants of development of the organization. These variants should present indicators used for development of strategic plans, defining quality of products and services, carrying out research and development work. In the forecast the innovative tasks on all structure of control system are developed and estimated. Innovations are predicted mainly with the help of methods of expert evaluations and modeling. Forecasts differ in: time of action - short-term (up to one year), medium-term (up to five years) and long-term (more than 20 years); scale of action - global, national, regional; types of innovation activities - product, process, strategic.
