Corporate culture. constructive and effective interaction between partners of an innovative organization. The resulting environment of communication contributes to the expansion of the space of creativity and the creation of conditions for self-realization of partners in corporate activities. It is based on traditions stemming from universal values that embody the values of culture of the multinational Russian people, unite people and help each member of the team to be aware of their commonality with others. The corporate culture is formed, among other things, when events relating to each member of the team are celebrated together. This creates an informal atmosphere that brings people together. This enriches intra-organizational communication and communication with the external environment, and social and work-related problems are resolved more quickly. The organization creates an environment which, in all its manifestations, promotes trust in each other, creativity and, as a consequence, innovation.
