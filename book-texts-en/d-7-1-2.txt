Classification of sources of financing innovations by types of property. Sources of financing are divided into: a) state investment resources (budget funds, extra-budgetary funds, state borrowings, shareholdings, state property); b) investment resources of economic entities of commercial and non-commercial orientation, as well as public organizations, individuals, etc.; c) c) investment resources of collective investors, as well as investment funds and companies, non-state pension funds, insurance companies, enterprises' own funds, credit resources of commercial banks, other credit institutions and investment banking structures specially authorized by the government.
