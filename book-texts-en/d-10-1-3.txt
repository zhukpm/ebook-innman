Managerial solution. an act of will of the manager used as a means of eliminating internal contradictions arising in the process of development of an innovative organization, the accumulation of which threatens chaos and loss of management. The decision-making process streamlines the structure of the organization and brings it into manageable development. The purpose of the decision is to mobilize and focus all available resources to bring the innovative organization into a better state (in line with its goals and objectives).
