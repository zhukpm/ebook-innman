Innovative culture. a set of innovative achievements of society based on the traditions of the Russian people, reflecting their desire for education, knowledge, development of non-standard thinking, use of natural intuitive abilities, creativity and teamwork, as well as such qualities of the Russian people as receptivity to innovation, tolerance to others and different worldviews. Skillful use of these traditions and qualities of people in innovative activity provides outstripping growth of spiritual and intellectual capital of organization, promotes an active increase in the number of innovations and their competitiveness. In this case formed a new quality of the results of innovative activity of the organization, which dominates the social, spiritual over material, ensuring the sustainability of its functioning.
