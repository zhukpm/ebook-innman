Analysis of the problem situation. a research method that helps to identify the causes of the situation; determine the degree of its novelty, the relationship with other problems; establish measures of solvability of the problem, especially from the perspective of information and resource provision. Various methods are used to analyze the situation and select options. The most widespread are methods of mathematical statistics, game theory, probability theory, linear and non-linear programming, simulation models, graph theory, queues, mass maintenance. It is important to ensure the proper balance in the use of modern analytical methods and subjective judgments in the practice of making innovative decisions.
