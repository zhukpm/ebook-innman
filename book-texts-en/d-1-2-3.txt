Functions of the spiritual sphere. a) preservation, enrichment, reproduction of the basic values of the people arising from its millenian culture and their use in all spheres of vital activity of the person (in personal life, family, industrial, social-political, public-power); b) mobilization of the people on execution of the purpose uniting it on achievement of true target orientations of development and consolidation of space of the state in the integral power, based on the uniform values; c) rendering of essential influence on forming of the public consciousness.
