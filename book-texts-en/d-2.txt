Topic summary.


The availability of spiritual, industrial-economics, socio-political and public-power conditions allows the use of such important components of innovation management as innovations and innovation potential. Innovation management in the public sphere and business, while serving the innovation sphere, contributes to the spread of innovations and the transformation of knowledge into a concrete result. It is based on innovation policy, the concept of scientific and technological development, the philosophy of innovation organization and realized through innovation activities, innovative processes, by using the innovative potential.
There are various sources of innovation that are the beginning of innovation.
There can be countless innovations in the public sphere, in business and civil society. They are recognized by their inherent attributes and differ significantly in scale, purpose, impact and efficiency. Innovations and innovation potential are revitalized by innovation policies and activities.
