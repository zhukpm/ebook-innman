Topic summary.

Innovative management solutions are diverse and complex. They can be intuitive, hypothetical individual, local, global, predictive, prospective and others. For working out innovative solutions necessary preconditions should be created. Solutions can be effective if their developers will observe necessary requirements and apply effective methods. Information is a key factor in making and implementing innovative management decisions. Innovative management decisions are an act of will of the head and underpin the creation and application of breakthrough technologies in practice.
